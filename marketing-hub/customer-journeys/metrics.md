# Metrics

## Default metrics

<table><thead><tr><th width="229">Metric</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Sent </td><td>Count the number of messages that the system has requested (the 3rd-party) to send to audiences</td></tr><tr><td>Delivered</td><td>Count the number of times that a message -sending-request has been replied to with status success</td></tr><tr><td>Delivered Rate</td><td>The delivery success rate is calculated by the formula:<br><code>Delivered Rate = Delivered/ Sent * 100</code></td></tr><tr><td>Bounces &#x26; Errors</td><td><p>Count the number of Bounces &#x26; Errors<br>(the messages delivered failed), including causes:</p><ul><li><em>Invalid send requests:</em> Requested content from CDP 365 is not valid for third-party requesting</li><li><em>Connection timeout errors:</em> Request body from CDP 365 sent to third party failed due to connection error</li><li><em>Hard Bounce &#x26; Soft Bounce:</em> Third-party error failed to allocate content to object</li></ul><p>It is calculated by the formula:</p><p><code>Bounces &#x26; Errors = Sent - Delivered</code></p></td></tr><tr><td>Bounces &#x26; Errors Rate</td><td><p>It is calculated by the formula:</p><p><code>Bounce &#x26; Error Rate = Bounces &#x26; Errors/ Sent * 100</code></p></td></tr><tr><td>Invalid sent requests</td><td>Send-request invalid because missing required info, incorrect info (the third party requesting)</td></tr><tr><td>Hard bounces</td><td>Hard bounces are the result of a permanent problem such as an invalid, closed, or non-existent recipient's address, e.g email address or phone number, and these messages will never be successfully delivered</td></tr><tr><td>Soft bounces</td><td>Soft bounces are the result of a temporary problem (with an already valid recipient's address,e.g valid email) such as a full inbox or a problem with the recipient’s server</td></tr><tr><td>Opens</td><td>Number of times the audience opened the message</td></tr><tr><td>Open Rate</td><td>The percentage of the audience opened message is calculated by the formula:<br><code>Open Rate = Opens/ Delivered * 100</code></td></tr><tr><td>Clicks</td><td>Number of times the audience clicked on the allocated message</td></tr><tr><td>Click Rate</td><td>The percentage of the audience who clicked on the allocated message is calculated by the formula:<br><code>Click Rate = Clicks/ Delivered * 100</code></td></tr><tr><td>CTR (Click through rate)</td><td>The Click-through rate is calculated by the formula:<br><code>CTR = Clicks/ Opens * 100</code></td></tr></tbody></table>

## Specific metrics&#x20;

### Web Personalization

<table><thead><tr><th width="232">Metric</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Viewable</td><td>Number of times the web campaign might be viewed</td></tr><tr><td>Viewable Rate</td><td>It is calculated by the formula: <br><code>Viewable rate = Viewable/ Delivered*100</code></td></tr><tr><td>CTR (Clicks/Viewable)</td><td>The Click-through rate is calculated by the formula:<br><code>CTR = Clicks/ Viewable * 100</code></td></tr><tr><td>Clicks</td><td>Number of times the audience clicked on the link attached in the sent message</td></tr><tr><td>Impressions</td><td>The number of times the web campaign was displayed</td></tr><tr><td>Click Rate</td><td>The percentage of the audience who clicked on the allocated message is calculated by the formula:<br><code>Click Rate = Clicks/ Delivered * 100</code></td></tr><tr><td>Checkout Cart</td><td>Number of times the audience checkout cart (a transaction is created) which comes from journey/campaign</td></tr><tr><td>Purchase</td><td>Number of times the audience purchased which comes from journey/campaign</td></tr><tr><td>View Product</td><td>Number of times the audience viewed a product that comes from journey/campaign</td></tr><tr><td>Add To Cart</td><td>Number of times the audience added products to the cart that comes from journey/campaign</td></tr><tr><td>Conversion Rate (Purchase/Click)</td><td>It is calculated by the formula:<br><code>Conversion Rate = Purchase / Click*100</code></td></tr><tr><td>Total revenue</td><td>The revenue that brings from the journey/campaign</td></tr></tbody></table>
